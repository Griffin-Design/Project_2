Step 1

How To Draw The Guidelines

Head
Start with a circle, then make a cross through it.
Spine
From the head, draw a line down the page.
Body
Under the head draw a box, then lower closer to the base of the spine draw another box.
Limbs
On the sides of both boxes draw two long lines.
