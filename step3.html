Step 3

How To Add More Features / Form

Eyes
Place simple circles on the head cross, adjust size and spacing.
Nose
Draw a small triangle or oval at the tip of the snout for the nose.
Tails
Add the tail shape using the spine guideline — simple curve or tuft.
Ears
Sketch ear shapes on top of the head circle and refine their placement.
